So, what's the Salt Shakerz podcast all about?

Salt Shakerz is the podcast for young Australian women who are seeking a grounded, biblically-rooted perspective on life's struggles.

Our mission is to foster freedom in identity and life through candid, biblically-centered conversations that resonate with the real-life struggles of young women. We aim to provide an authentic space where you can explore what it means to be truly seen, loved, and valued in Christ—just as you are—and how that can influence your life. Through stories of imperfect faith and personal growth, we’re here to remind you that grace is a journey and that God’s truth speaks to every part of who you are, even the parts you feel might not “measure up.”

Meet your passionate hosts.

Hi, I'm Ebony!

I live in Perth, and if there’s one thing you should know about me, it’s that I'm 100% a cat lady. My two cats, Mary and Martha, rule my house—and honestly, I wouldn’t have it any other way.

Creativity has always been a huge part of my life, whether I’m sketching, filming, or finding new ways to make a mess with paint. But more than anything, my faith in Jesus is what shapes me. I genuinely don’t know where I’d be without Him. The fact that we have a God who isn’t distant but personal—who gives us purpose, comforts us, and actually wants a relationship with us? That’s always been incredible to me.

I have such a heart for seeing women grow in their faith and break free from the battles in their minds. I know what it’s like to wrestle with self-doubt and overthinking, and while I’m definitely not an expert, I believe in the power of honest conversations. That’s why I believe God placed it on my heart to started The Salt Shakerz with Esther—to create a space where we can talk about faith, identity, and all the real struggles, without pretending we have it all together.

Thanks for reading.
