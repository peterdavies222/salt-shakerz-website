Episode 1:

Pilot: shaking up the heart

Welcome to Salt Shakerz!

Get to know us in this pilot episode as we share the vision for the podcast, and discuss the heart behind our words and actions in starting this project.

Jump into Matthew 6:1-4 as we find out what Jesus has to say about being genuine with our intentions.

36:58

Episode 2:

Shaking Up Beauty Standards: Where do we find our worth?

What is true beauty? How does God see us? In this episode, we get real about our personal struggles with self-esteem, the pressure to look a certain way, and where we’ve placed our value.

We also unpack questions like: Is makeup bad to wear as a Christian? How should we approach outward appearance with the right heart posture? And… are boyfriend checklists a thing?

Disclaimer: Take everything with a grain of salt! :) We know that everyone has their own personal journey, and we’re not experts—just two girls figuring it out as we go.
